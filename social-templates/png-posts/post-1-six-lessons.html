6 lessons every founder should steal.

1

A slow site = a dead brand.
Speed isn't a feature. It's a promise.

2

3

"Just make it pop" is the most expensive sentence in design. Clarity > Creativity.

4

SEO isn't dead. Lazy SEO is. One pillar page beats 50 thin posts.

5

The best UX is invisible. If users have to think, you've already lost them.

6

Marketing doesn't fix a broken product. Fix the product. Then turn up the volume.
