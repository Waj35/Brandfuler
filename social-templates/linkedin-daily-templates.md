## 🧠 CAPTION FORMULA (Use for any template)

```
HOOK    →  Stop the scroll (Line 1, max 8 words)
CONTEXT →  Why this matters (Line 2-3)
BODY    →  The meat (3-7 short lines or bullets)
LESSON  →  The "so what" (1-2 lines)
CTA     →  Comment / Save / DM / Visit
```

**Formatting rules that boost reach:**
- Line 1 must hook hard. No throat-clearing.
- Use **white space**. Single sentences = single paragraphs.
- Emojis as punctuation, not decoration. Max 3-5 per post.
- End with a question or a clear CTA. Always.
- Cap posts at 1,300 characters for max engagement.

## 📌 QUICK-START WORKFLOW (Daily, ~5 mins)

1. Open this file.
2. Check the calendar — pick today's template.
3. Fill the `[BRACKETS]` based on what's on your mind / a recent project / news.
4. Copy → paste into LinkedIn.
5. Add 5-10 hashtags from the bank (always include the 3 core).
6. Post between **8-10 AM** or **5-7 PM** (highest LinkedIn engagement).
7. Reply to first 5 comments within 30 mins (algo rewards this hard).

---

## 🚀 PRO MOVES (Do these once you're consistent)

- **Pin your best post** to the top of your profile.
- **Repost top-performers** every 6-8 weeks (audience won't notice; reach grows).
- **Tag 1-2 relevant people** in genuine context (not spam).
- **Carousel posts get 3x reach** of single images on LinkedIn — use Template 9 weekly.
- **Comment on 5 bigger creators' posts** *before* you post yours daily.
